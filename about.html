About Me




Sadaf Faraz is a highly-motivated, hard working, creative woman who was born and raised in Northern Virginia to Afghan immigrants. Due to her love for traveling and curiousity of other countries, she studied and graduated with a B.A. in Government and International Politics from George Mason University. The passionate web designer is always eager to learn new things and enjoys the challenge of solving complex problems. She even handcoded this website from scratch!
